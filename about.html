Who We Are

Founded in 2008 by water engineer John Booker, Booker Investment Group began as a small drilling operation in Kampala and has grown into one of East Africa's most respected water solutions providers. Our journey has been marked by innovation, quality service, and a deep commitment to solving water challenges.

What began with a single drilling rig and three employees has expanded to a fleet of 12 modern drilling rigs and a team of 85 professionals including hydrogeologists, civil engineers, and water system specialists. We've completed projects in 12 countries across the region.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable, innovative water solutions that improve lives and empower communities through reliable access to clean water.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading water solutions company, recognized for technical excellence, environmental stewardship, and social impact.

Our Advanced Equipment

We invest heavily in modern equipment to ensure efficient, high-quality results for every project. Our fleet includes:

  • 5 Atlas Copco drilling rigs (300m capacity)
  • 3 Schramm T685WS top-head drive rigs
  • 2 Geoprobe 6620DT direct push rigs
  • 12 Support vehicles and trailers
  • Geophysical survey equipment including ABEM Terrameter

All equipment is regularly maintained and upgraded to meet international standards. Our investment in technology allows us to work in diverse terrains from hard rock to alluvial formations.
