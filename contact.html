Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Borehole |Road |Housing project take?

Most standard borehole projects take 2-4 weeks from survey to completion, depending on depth and geology. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

What's the average cost of a Borehole |Road |Housing?

Costs vary based on depth and location, ranging from $3,000 for a shallow well to $15,000+ for deep boreholes with complete installations. We offer transparent quotes with no hidden costs.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance packages including pump servicing, water quality testing, and system inspections to ensure long-term performance.
